Introduction

The Central Ordering System for Hospitals is a software designed to streamline appointment scheduling and order management within hospitals. This system provides four distinct roles: Doctor of General Medicine, Specialist Doctor, and Patient, each with specific capabilities and responsibilities within the system.

Roles and Capabilities

Doctor of General Medicine:

Create patients.
Ad patient orders.
Schedule appointments for patients.

Specialist Doctor:

Modify orders.
Send feedback to the Doctor of General Medicine and patients.

Patient:

Check their orders.

System Features

User Authentication:

Users need to log in using their credentials to access the system and perform tasks according to their roles.

Patient Management:

Allows Doctor of General Medicine to create records.
Allows patients to view their orders.

Order Management:

Enables Doctor of General Medicine to add orders.

Appointment Scheduling:

Permits Doctor of General Medicine to schedule appointments for patients.

Feedback System:

Allows Specialist Doctors to send feedback to Doctor of General Medicine and patients.

Usage Instructions

Login:

Upon accessing the system, users will be prompted to log in using their designated credentials for their respective roles.

Administrator:

Use the administrative dashboard to see any items within the system, including user accounts, appointments, and orders.

Doctor of General Medicine:

Access the patient management module to create, modify, or delete patient records.
Utilize the order management module to add orders.
Schedule appointments for patients using the appointment scheduling feature.

Specialist Doctor:

Utilize the feedback system to provide feedback to Doctor of General Medicine and patients.
